Olivia Chow Releases 2026 City Budget.

Mayor Olivia Chow has released the final version of her 2026 city budget.

She is adding new measures for small businesses and a downtown safety initiative to the spending plan.

The spending plan goes to counsil this month.

Chow says that the last budget of her three-year term is about “delivering an affordable, caring, and safe city.”

This Once Popular Product is being Discontinued in Canada

What was once a staple in many Canadian homes will soon be unavailable in stores.

Minute Maid frozen juice concentrate will be discontinued in Canada and the U.S.

The company is “exiting the frozen can category” entirely in Canada, it said in an emailed statement.

The decision was made “in response to shifting consumer preferences.”

Ozzy Has the Longest Tongue of Any Living Dog

The French and bull mastiff cross in Oklahoma has a tongue measuring 7.8 inches.

The previous record for the longest tongue was just over five inches.

Ozzy has really licked the competition in this unusual category.

He is an incredibly cute dog.

You might be asking - isn't that two words?

The publisher says it's not tricking you.

He adds that it's one word when used as a hyphenated compound phrase.

Bighorn sheep rescued from frozen river in Colorado

Wildlife officials in Colorado came to the rescue of a bighorn sheep that wandered out onto a frozen river.

The sheep was loaded onto a trailer and taken to a facility to warm up.

The ewe was reunited with the rest of her herd on Tuesday.

"She bolted out of the horse trailer in excellent health, a relief to our entire Gunnison area office staff," officials wrote.

Kangaroo found hopping loose down Texas road

Authorities in Texas are trying to find the owner of a kangaroo found hopping down the middle of a Liberty County road.

The officers and animal control personnel were able to corral the marsupial with help from members of the public.

Police are now attempting to find the kangaroo's owner.

Anyone with information on the animal's origins is being asked to contact the Liberty County Sheriff's Office.

2-year-old breaks two world records playing pool and snooker

A toddler pool prodigy in England has already broken two Guinness World Records for playing pool and snooker.

Jude Owens broke the record for the youngest person to make a snooker double pot at the age of 2 years and 261 days old.

The boy's father, Luke Owens, said he knew Jude would excel at the sport from the first time he picked up a cue.

Jude has achieved quite a lot in such a short space of time.
